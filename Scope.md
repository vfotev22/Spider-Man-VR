Game Length: 30 mins

Levels: The player will play within one level/stage throughout the game which will be a large open city.

Average Playtime: 10 mins

Objectives: The player will be tasked with going around the city to specific locations in order to take pictures with a camera of various scenes going on. Possibly other objectives could include the player being tasked to stop criminals. 

Playable Characters: There will be one playable character which is Spiderman or a Spiderman adjacent character.

Customization/Upgrades: Better webslingers for traversing and cosmetic costumes.
